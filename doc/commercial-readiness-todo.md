## Contract Documents To Prepare

- [ ] Master Services Agreement for bespoke software development, consulting,
      integration, and implementation work.
- [ ] Statement of Work template covering scope, deliverables, assumptions,
      timelines, fees, expenses, acceptance, dependencies, and change control.
- [ ] Product or SaaS Terms for any hosted platform, dashboard, API, or
      subscription product.
- [ ] Data Processing Agreement for customer personal data processed by XeonTek.
- [ ] Acceptable Use Policy for hosted products, APIs, and interactive systems.
- [ ] SLA and support terms covering uptime targets, exclusions, support hours,
      response targets, maintenance windows, and remedies.
- [ ] Security addendum covering access control, encryption, logging, incident
      response, vulnerability handling, backup, business continuity, and audit
      cooperation.
- [ ] Subprocessor list covering provider name, purpose, location, transfer
      mechanism, and customer notification process.
- [ ] AI product terms covering AI-assisted outputs, human review obligations,
      prohibited uses, model limitations, data use, evaluation, monitoring, and
      regulatory disclosures.
