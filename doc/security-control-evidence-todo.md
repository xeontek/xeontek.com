# ISO 27001 And SOC 2 Readiness TODO

This is an internal evidence checklist. It should not be treated as a statement
that XeonTek is ISO/IEC 27001 certified, ISO/IEC 27001 compliant, SOC 2
certified, SOC 2 audited, or SOC 2 compliant.

## Scope And Governance

- [ ] Define the organisation, products, systems, locations, and people in
      scope.
- [ ] Assign control owners and review cadence.
- [ ] Maintain an asset inventory for systems, repositories, cloud accounts,
      domains, devices, and critical SaaS providers.
- [ ] Maintain a data inventory and data classification scheme.
- [ ] Maintain a risk register with treatment decisions and owners.

## Access Control

- [ ] Define joiner, mover, and leaver access processes.
- [ ] Require MFA for critical systems.
- [ ] Perform periodic access reviews for source control, Cloudflare, email,
      production systems, and finance/admin systems.
- [ ] Maintain privileged access approvals and evidence.

## Change Management

- [ ] Define code review, testing, approval, and deployment requirements.
- [ ] Keep deployment logs and release evidence.
- [ ] Track emergency change approvals and post-change reviews.

## Incident Response

- [ ] Maintain an incident response policy and contact rota.
- [ ] Define severity levels, escalation paths, customer notification criteria,
      and regulator notification criteria.
- [ ] Run at least one tabletop exercise and retain evidence.

## Vendor And Subprocessor Management

- [ ] Maintain a vendor/subprocessor register.
- [ ] Review provider security, privacy, and transfer terms before onboarding.
- [ ] Track renewal dates, owners, and offboarding steps.

## Data Protection And Retention

- [ ] Define retention schedules for customer data, recruitment data, support
      data, logs, and legal/compliance records.
- [ ] Document backup, restore, and deletion processes.
- [ ] Test restore procedures where hosted products or customer data are in
      scope.

## Security Operations

- [ ] Define vulnerability management and patching expectations.
- [ ] Track vulnerability reports and remediation decisions.
- [ ] Keep evidence of security monitoring and alert review where applicable.
- [ ] Maintain business continuity and disaster recovery plans for in-scope
      systems.

## Training And Awareness

- [ ] Deliver security awareness training to staff and contractors.
- [ ] Deliver AI literacy and responsible AI training for people involved in AI
      systems.
- [ ] Keep training completion evidence.

## External Assurance

- [ ] Decide whether to pursue ISO/IEC 27001 certification, SOC 2 Type I, SOC 2
      Type II, or a lighter customer security pack first.
- [ ] Select an auditor or certification body if formal assurance is required.
- [ ] Prepare customer-facing security statements only after scope and evidence
      are approved.
