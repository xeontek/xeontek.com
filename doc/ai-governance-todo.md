# AI Governance TODO

Internal checklist before making stronger public AI Act compliance claims.

- [ ] Maintain an inventory of AI systems and AI-assisted features.
- [ ] Classify each AI system under the EU AI Act risk framework.
- [ ] Review systems against prohibited AI practices.
- [ ] Complete a high-risk assessment checklist where relevant.
- [ ] Document intended purpose, users, limitations, and foreseeable misuse.
- [ ] Define human oversight responsibilities for each system.
- [ ] Document logging, monitoring, and incident handling requirements.
- [ ] Record data governance, evaluation, and model validation practices.
- [ ] Keep technical documentation proportionate to each system's risk.
- [ ] Provide AI literacy/training for staff involved in AI systems.
- [ ] Review financial, legal, and investment-related outputs for appropriate
      disclaimers and human review requirements.
