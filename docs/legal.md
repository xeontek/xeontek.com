# Legal And Compliance Notes

This file is an internal implementation reference, not legal advice. Public
legal wording should be reviewed by counsel where risk is material.

## Public Website Positioning

- Use the legal name `XeonTek Ltd` where formal identity is required.
- Include company registration details and registered office where legal
  disclosure is expected.
- Treat the public Terms as website terms only.
- Do not imply the website terms cover paid services, hosted products, APIs,
  bespoke development, SLAs, or data processing obligations.

## Privacy And Cookies

- The contact form should include a point-of-collection privacy notice.
- Privacy wording should cover contact enquiries, recruitment enquiries,
  Turnstile verification, Cloudflare hosting/security logs, Brevo transactional
  email delivery, and whitepaper/PDF request logs where relevant.
- The site should state that it does not use analytics cookies unless that
  changes.
- Cloudflare Turnstile may use cookies or similar technologies for bot and
  abuse prevention.
- Avoid adding a cookie banner unless non-essential cookies or analytics are
  introduced.

## AI Claims

- The public website does not provide an AI system to visitors.
- The website does not make automated decisions about visitors.
- Avoid implying regulated financial advice, investment advice, or automated
  investment decision-making.
- Product-specific AI disclosures should live with the relevant product or
  service documentation.

## Security And Assurance Claims

- Do not claim ISO/IEC 27001 certification, ISO/IEC 27001 compliance, SOC 2
  certification, SOC 2 audit completion, or SOC 2 compliance unless the scope
  and evidence are independently verified.
- Keep public security statements factual and limited to current controls.
- Route vulnerability reports to the published security contact.

## Documents Needed Before Commercial Products

- Master Services Agreement, if bespoke work is offered.
- Statement of Work template, if project work is offered.
- Product or SaaS Terms, if hosted products are offered.
- Data Processing Agreement, if customer personal data is processed.
- Acceptable Use Policy for hosted products or APIs.
- SLA/support terms, if uptime or support commitments are offered.
- Security addendum and subprocessor list.
- AI product terms for AI-assisted outputs, limitations, prohibited uses, data
  use, monitoring, and regulatory disclosures.
